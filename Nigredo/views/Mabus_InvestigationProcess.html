Investigation Process

Suspect gave most of the user names to each social media account,
however gathering the Facebook username, email address, and
Twitter account required some digging. Firstly, to obtain the
Facebook account, the suspect's Game Jolt account was consulted.
Then to obtain the email address, both the associated Twitter
account and his Facebook account were used to determine the
host part of the email address.
To obtain the suspect's middle initial, Discord logs were deployed
to determine the birth month of the suspect. Then with a combination
of the suspects first name and last name along with the birth-date
the New Jersey voter records were consulted to obtain the appropiate information.
